## Design Context

**Target audience**: Health-conscious individuals who want to look up glycemic index/load values while grocery shopping, cooking, or meal planning. Primary use on mobile phones.

**Use cases**: 
- Quick lookup: "What's the GI of sweet potatoes?" while at the store
- Comparison: "Which rice has the lowest GI?"
- Reference: checking back on recently viewed foods

**Brand personality/tone**: Clean, warm, pleasant — like a well-designed cookbook page. Not clinical or medical. Serene and trustworthy. The anti-thesis of ad-laden, cluttered GI apps.

**Aesthetic direction**: Warm & Earthy — cream/linen backgrounds, serif typography (Georgia), sage green / warm amber / terracotta accent colors for GI classification. White cards with subtle shadows. Feels editorial and understated.

**Key differentiator**: Ad-free, fast, works offline, not ugly. That's the entire value prop against existing GI apps.
